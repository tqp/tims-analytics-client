Auto-Complete Demo
This is a demo of auto-complete functionality from a database.
The "Last Name" and "Street Address" fields, marked in green, are the auto-complete fields.
When you begin typing into either of those fields, a drop-down will appear with the first 5 records in the MySQL database where there is a wildcard match.
If you click on one of the matches in the drop-down, the related fields will be filled in automatically.

Examples to try: Enter an 'r' in the Last Name field and select Leota Ragel. Or, enter the word 'lake' in the Address field and select a record.
